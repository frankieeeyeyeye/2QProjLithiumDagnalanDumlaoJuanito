HEADLINE

The Philippines’ overall inflation rate further decelerated to 3.9 percent in December 2023––the lowest rate recorded in 2023. “Our whole-of-government approach of maintaining price stability, implementing timely non-monetary policy measures, and addressing supply gaps have paid off. Rest assured that the Marcos, Jr. administration is fully prepared to further mitigate the effects of inflation this year amidst global headwinds,” Finance Secretary Benjamin E. Diokno said.
